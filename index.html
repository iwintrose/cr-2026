What's Worth Buying
Specific recommendations based on where you're going and what you'll be doing.
Sun & Water
Essential
Raw Elements Reef-Safe SPF 50
Mineral-based, certified reef-safe, effective in tropical sun. Destination law prohibits chemical-based sunscreens. This is the gold standard.
Available at MEC, Well.ca, or Amazon. Get the tin — lasts all week.
Essential
Sea to Summit Dry Bag 8L
For boat excursions and water activities. Your phone, wallet, and camera need protection — the water is not gentle.
Under $30 at MEC. Get the 8L — big enough for phone, wallet, small camera.
GoPro Hero or Waterproof Camera
If you're doing any snorkel excursion, the underwater visibility is extraordinary. You'll regret not capturing it.
Rental also available through most dive operators on-site.
UV50+ Rash Guard
UV index averages 11–12 in {{TRIP_MONTH}}. A rash guard for snorkelling and boat tours is more practical than reapplying sunscreen every hour in the water.
Also useful for zip-lining — keeps harness from scratching.
Health & Comfort
Recommended
LMNT Electrolyte Packets
Your destination averages 33°C with high humidity in {{TRIP_MONTH}}. Electrolyte packets taken every morning prevent headaches and fatigue that waste a vacation day.
Bring 10–12 packets. Available at most health stores or Amazon.
Stugeron Motion Tablets
For boat excursions. Seas can be choppy in the afternoon. Stugeron is far more effective than Gravol for serious motion sickness.
Available OTC at most pharmacies. Take one hour before boarding.
